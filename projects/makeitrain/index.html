Make it Rain

this is just a small project to add rain to your desktop. it's nothing complicated, and was completed within a day. enjoy :)

make sure to turn off interaction in übersicht's settings after installation.
note: when the widget first starts, it'll take over as the now playing app. to fix this, play and pause something in another app, so hitting the pause key on your keyboard doesn't stop the rain.
